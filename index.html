Top 10 Places to Visit in Chennai

Marina Beach

The second longest beach in the world, famous for its stunning sunrise and sunset.

Fort St. George

A historic fort built in 1644, now home to the Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly.

Kabaleeshwarar Temple

A beautiful Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its architecture.

Theosophical Society

A peaceful garden and library located in Adyar, known for its rich flora and fauna.

Government Museum

One of the oldest museums in India, featuring a vast collection of artifacts.

Birla Planetarium

An educational attraction that offers shows on astronomy and space science.

Elliot's Beach

A popular beach for relaxation, known for its peaceful ambiance and food stalls.

Valluvar Kottam

A monument dedicated to the Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar, featuring intricate carvings.

Mahabalipuram

A UNESCO World Heritage Site known for its rock-cut temples and stunning shore temple.

Chennai Trade Centre

A hub for exhibitions and trade shows, showcasing local and international products.
